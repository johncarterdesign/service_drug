Get your flu shots at Service Drug

We offer the Flu shot and other vaccinations as a walk-in service anytime during business hours.

The CDC has reported estimating conservatively that the influenza vaccination prevented 79,000 hospitalizations and 6.6 million illnesses last season. Now is the time to protect yourself and your family.

We can bill most insurances, including insured employees of IPSC, Materion, Intermountain Health, Millard School District and most Millard County plans. We can also bill other private insurances and Medicare. Insurance companies, including Medicare, usually cover the Flu vaccine at 100%.

Come in and talk with our pharmacist or nurse about your concerns and questions. Receive a 10% coupon for items in the store on the day of your vaccination and enter our drawing for a $25 gift card given out weekly to those who receive a vaccination.

Delivery service

We understand that getting your medications in rural areas can be a challenge.

That's why we offer home prescription delivery to our customers in Leamington, Lynndyl, Oak City and East Millard County. We also will deliver and set up medical equipment throughout the county.
